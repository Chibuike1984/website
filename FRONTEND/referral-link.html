How does referring a friend work?

When you refer a friend using your link or username, they must sign up and complete a qualifying transaction. Then, you both may receive bonuses.

How fast do I get my bonus after my friend signs up and makes a payment?

If your friend used your referral link or username to sign up and made a qualifying transaction, you should receive your bonus within 48 hours. Sometimes up to 5 business days. Requirements:

  • Minimum transfer of $150 (USD, CAD, GBP, EUR).
  • Must be funded by a bank/card/partner wallet.
  • Excludes same currency transfers.
  • Completed within 30 days of signup.
